I am a PhD candidate in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the University of Ottawa. My research interests include XR, healthcare, computer vision, and multimedia to improve users' QoE and facilitate citizens' well-being using advanced signal processing and machine learning algorithms, such as CNN, transformer, LLM and reinforcement learning. I have led on several projects, such as emotion detection, vital signs estimation, exercise recognition and counting, 3D Avatar reconstruction and XR optimization, and published multiple papers in prestigious journals. I am passionate about applying my skills and knowledge to solve real-world problems and create positive social impact.
